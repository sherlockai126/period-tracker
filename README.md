A simple, privacy-focused web application to help track menstrual cycles, set reminders, and log symptoms.

## Features

- **Period Date Recording**: Record when your period starts each month
- **Smart Reminders**: Get notified 3 days before your next expected period with preparation tips
- **Symptom Logging**: Keep track of period-related symptoms and patterns
- **Cycle Analytics**: View your average cycle length and period history
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally in your browser - no servers involved

## How to Use

1. **Record Period Dates**: Enter the start date of your last period
2. **Log Symptoms**: Note any symptoms you experience with dates
3. **Check Reminders**: The app will automatically calculate and display upcoming period predictions
4. **View History**: Track your cycle patterns over time

## Preparation Reminders Include:
- Stock up on pads/tampons
- Keep warm clothes ready
- Have warm water/tea available
- Stay prepared for comfort items

## Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser or visit the live version at: https://sherlockai126.github.io/period-tracker/

## Technical Details

- **Frontend Only**: Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- **Local Storage**: Data persists in your browser's local storage
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **No Server Required**: Complete privacy - no data leaves your device

## Privacy & Security

- All data is stored locally in your browser
- No personal information is sent to any servers
- You have complete control over your data
- Clear your browser data to remove all tracked information
